Private Dining

The Whole Restaurant, Just for You

Some moments deserve a space entirely your own. At La Botte D'Oro, we offer exclusive use of the entire restaurant for your private events — creating an atmosphere that feels warm, personal, and unmistakably Italian.

Our owner Gabriele and his dedicated team work closely with you to craft a custom menu, manage every detail, and ensure your guests experience the same legendary warmth that has made us a Leichhardt institution since 1977.

Whether you're planning a special lunch for 60 or an intimate dinner for 20, we'll make it feel exactly right.

  • Exclusive venue hire — full restaurant available for private events
  • Bespoke menus tailored to your occasion and dietary needs
  • Dedicated event coordinator to manage every detail
  • Flexible set menus or à la carte dining for groups
  • Full bar service with curated Italian wine list
  • Family-friendly — high chairs, pram access, and genuinely welcoming
  • Short-notice bookings accommodated wherever possible
