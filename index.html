47 Years of Tradition
Our Story

Leichhardt's Italian Restaurant Since 1977

La Botte D'Oro has been at the heart of Leichhardt's Italian community for over 47 years. Founded in 1977 on the belief that food is love made visible, this Italian restaurant in Leichhardt has always been more than a place to eat — it's a home away from home.

Our owner Gabriele greets every guest with the warm hospitality that has defined us across nearly five decades. At the heart of our kitchen sits Nonna's philosophy — use only the finest ingredients, cook with your whole heart, and never rush a good thing.

We honour traditional Italian cooking with a touch of modern inspiration: slow-braised ragù, hand-rolled pasta, and pizzas from our wood-fired oven — the same flavours Leichhardt has loved since the day we opened our doors.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit to Leichhardt's favourite Italian restaurant.

What is the best Italian restaurant in Leichhardt?

La Botte D'Oro at 137 Marion Street is Leichhardt's most consistently praised Italian restaurant, with a 4.7-star rating across 625+ Google reviews. Open since 1977, guests return for the handmade pasta, wood-fired pizzas, and the warm hospitality of owner Gabriele — the kind of place where every visit feels like coming home.

What are La Botte D'Oro's opening hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 7:30 am – 3:00 pm and again from 5:30 pm – 10:00 pm. On weekends, breakfast and lunch run 8:00 am – 3:00 pm, and dinner from 5:30 pm – 10:30 pm. The restaurant closes between 3:00 pm and 5:30 pm every day. Call us on (02) 9560 1349 to confirm.

Does La Botte D'Oro offer private dining and group bookings?

Yes — we regularly host private events for groups of 20 to 60+ guests, including the full restaurant exclusively for your party. We've hosted christenings, anniversary dinners, birthday celebrations, Valentine's Day set menus, and corporate lunches. Learn more about private dining or call Gabriele directly on (02) 9560 1349.

Does La Botte D'Oro offer takeaway or delivery?

Yes. We offer dine-in, kerbside pickup, and no-contact home delivery. Takeaway pizzas are a popular option — call ahead on (02) 9560 1349 or order online.

Is La Botte D'Oro family-friendly? Are prams welcome?

Absolutely. La Botte D'Oro is genuinely, warmly family-friendly. We accommodate prams, high chairs, and short-notice family bookings without fuss. Gabriele and the team believe a great Italian restaurant should welcome everyone — bambini included.

What should I order at La Botte D'Oro?

First-time visitors should not leave without trying the Pappardelle Bolognese (Nonna's slow-braised meat sauce on handmade pasta), the wood-fired Margherita pizza, and the crema di caffè with your morning coffee. Other crowd favourites include the homemade lamb ragù, the Prosciutto e Burrata pizza, fresh cannoli, and perfectly cooked Barramundi. See our full menu here.
