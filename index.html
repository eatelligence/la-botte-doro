47 Years of Tradition
Our Story

A Family Table, Open to Everyone

La Botte D'Oro has been a cornerstone of Leichhardt's Italian community since 1977. Founded on the belief that food is love made visible, our restaurant has always been more than just a place to eat — it's a home away from home.

Our owner Gabriele welcomes every guest with the same warm hospitality that has defined us for nearly five decades. At the heart of our kitchen is Nonna's philosophy — use the finest ingredients, cook with your whole heart, and never rush a good thing.

We bring together traditional Italian flavours with a touch of modern inspiration, honouring the classics while keeping things exciting. Whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth, we promise it will feel like coming home.

Morning Ritual

Il Caffè del Mattino

There's something magical about a morning in Italy — the clatter of espresso cups, the sweet smell of a fresh cornetto, the barista who knows your order before you open your mouth.

At La Botte D'Oro, our superstar barista has brought that exact feeling to Leichhardt. Come in for the best cappuccino in Sydney, freshly baked pastries, and a moment that takes you straight back to standing at a bar in Rome or Naples.

Whether you're a regular or a newcomer, every morning coffee here is a small escape to Italy — without the airfare.

"The crema di caffè alone is worth the trip. It genuinely takes you back to being in Italy — having your morning coffee at the bar."

— A regular at La Botte D'Oro
